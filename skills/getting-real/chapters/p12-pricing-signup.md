# Pricing and Signup

## When to Load This

Load this when the product needs a cleaner entry path, more trust at signup, or a less gimmicky conversion model.

## What This Part Covers

- Chapter 64: Free Samples
- Chapter 65: Easy On, Easy Off
- Chapter 66: Silly Rabbit, Tricks are for Kids
- Chapter 67: A Softer Bullet

## Core Idea

Let people taste value with low friction. Make signup and cancellation humane, and avoid manipulative tricks that weaken trust.

## Chapter Routing

- **Ch 64 Free Samples** -> Let prospects experience the product before a heavy commitment.
- **Ch 65 Easy On, Easy Off** -> Reduce friction both entering and leaving.
- **Ch 66 Silly Rabbit, Tricks are for Kids** -> Drop cheap conversion gimmicks.
- **Ch 67 A Softer Bullet** -> Use gentler calls to action and more trust-building language.

## Practical Reads

- If conversion depends on pressure, the product may not be convincing enough on its own.
- Lower-friction signup often teaches more than elaborate persuasion flows.
- Trust compounds. Tricks cash it out early.
