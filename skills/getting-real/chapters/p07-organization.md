# The Organization

## When to Load This

Load this when team dynamics, communication overhead, or meeting culture are hurting output.

## What This Part Covers

- Chapter 36: Unity
- Chapter 37: Alone Time
- Chapter 38: Meetings Are Toxic
- Chapter 39: Seek and Celebrate Small Victories

## Core Idea

Great product work needs alignment, uninterrupted time, and momentum. Bureaucratic coordination kills all three.

## Chapter Routing

- **Ch 36 Unity** -> The team is fragmented or working at cross-purposes.
- **Ch 37 Alone Time** -> Makers need uninterrupted blocks to think and build.
- **Ch 38 Meetings Are Toxic** -> Meetings are replacing progress.
- **Ch 39 Seek and Celebrate Small Victories** -> Morale and momentum need visible wins.

## Practical Reads

- If calendars are full, the product is probably starving.
- Small wins matter because they compound confidence and proof.
- This part is blunt: many meetings are institutionalized interruption.
