or product-led promotion loops.

## Core Idea

Promotion is part storytelling, part teaching, part product design. Launch with intention, explain the value clearly, and let the product create more demand from inside itself.

## Chapter Routing

- **Ch 68 Hollywood Launch** -> Need a deliberate launch moment, not a shrug.
- **Ch 69 A Powerful Promo Site** -> The marketing site is weak or vague.
- **Ch 70 Ride the Blog Wave** -> Use ongoing writing and audience-building.
- **Ch 71 Solicit Early** -> Gather interest before the full release.
- **Ch 72 Promote Through Education** -> Teach the market instead of only pitching.
- **Ch 73 Feature Food** -> Package features as digestible stories.
- **Ch 74 Track Your Logs** -> Learn from actual traffic and behavior signals.
- **Ch 75 Inline Upsell** -> Surface upgrade value in context.
- **Ch 76 Name Hook** -> Product naming and memorability matter.
