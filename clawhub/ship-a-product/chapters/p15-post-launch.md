# Post-Launch

## When to Load This

Load this after release, when the question is how to improve without bloating,
how to handle bugs, or how to keep momentum alive.

## What This Part Covers

- Chapter 83: One Month Tuneup
- Chapter 84: Keep the Posts Coming
- Chapter 85: Better, Not Beta
- Chapter 86: All Bugs Are Not Created Equal
- Chapter 87: Ride Out the Storm
- Chapter 88: Keep Up With the Joneses
- Chapter 89: Beware the Bloat Monster
- Chapter 90: Go With The Flow

## Core Idea

The first release is the beginning of real learning. Tune up what matters, keep communicating, triage bugs intelligently, and resist the slow creep back into bloat.

## Chapter Routing

- **Ch 83 One Month Tuneup** -> Schedule a focused cleanup pass after launch.
- **Ch 84 Keep the Posts Coming** -> Keep writing and communicating after release.
- **Ch 85 Better, Not Beta** -> Ship something you are willing to stand behind.
- **Ch 86 All Bugs Are Not Created Equal** -> Prioritize bugs by actual impact.
- **Ch 87 Ride Out the Storm** -> Launch backlash or turbulence needs composure.
- **Ch 88 Keep Up With the Joneses** -> Pay attention to the market without becoming a clone.
- **Ch 89 Beware the Bloat Monster** -> Guard against feature creep after success.
- **Ch 90 Go With the Flow** -> Let usage teach the next direction.

## Practical Reads

- Post-launch is where discipline either compounds or dies.
- Not every bug matters equally, and not every request deserves code.
- The product should evolve, but not by panic.
