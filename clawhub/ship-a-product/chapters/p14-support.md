# Support

## When to Load This

Load this when users are confused, angry, lost, or asking for help,
and the team needs a saner support philosophy.

## What This Part Covers

- Chapter 77: Feel The Pain
- Chapter 78: Zero Training
- Chapter 79: Answer Quick
- Chapter 80: Tough Love
- Chapter 81: In Fine Forum
- Chapter 82: Publicize Your Screwups

## Core Idea

Support should keep the team close to user pain, push the product toward self-explanation, and respond fast without becoming fake or defensive.

## Chapter Routing

- **Ch 77 Feel The Pain** -> The builders are insulated from support reality.
- **Ch 78 Zero Training** -> The product should teach itself as much as possible.
- **Ch 79 Answer Quick** -> Speed matters in support because silence multiplies frustration.
- **Ch 80 Tough Love** -> Not every demand should be indulged.
- **Ch 81 In Fine Forum** -> Shared spaces can reduce repeat support and build community knowledge.
- **Ch 82 Publicize Your Screwups** -> Be honest about mistakes and fixes.

## Practical Reads

- If support is outsourced too far from the product team, this file matters.
- Fast, honest responses often beat polished, delayed ones.
- Good support is product research wearing a different hat.
